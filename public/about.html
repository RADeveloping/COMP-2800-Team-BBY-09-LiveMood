About Us


LiveMood is a small company operating out of Vancouver, BC. The COVID-19 pandemic has had a negative impact on countless lives and left many people without work or support. At LiveMood we foster an atmosphere of inclusion and respect. We inspire each other everyday to be better versions of ourselves. Our mission is to bring people together during these trying times, and promote support among peers.
